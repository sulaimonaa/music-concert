Welcome To The

Night of Recovery Concert

The Night of Recovery Concert is a celebration of strength, resilience, and the power of music in healing. This captivating event brings together renowned musicians, inspiring speakers, and a community of individuals who believe in the transformative power of recovery.

May 26th, 2023

Main Program

Inspirational Performances

Immerse yourself in soul-stirring performances by some of the most talented artists from various genres.

Inspiring Stories

Prepare to be moved by powerful stories of triumph and recovery shared by individuals who have overcome addiction.

Community Support

This event provides a safe space for individuals, families, and communities affected by addiction to come together and show their support.

Educational Resources

Gain valuable insights into addiction, recovery, and mental health through informative presentations and resources available at the event.

Silent Auction

Participate in our silent auction and get a chance to win unique items and experiences.
